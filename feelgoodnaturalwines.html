Feel Good Natural Wines

A Native IOS and Android App for Browsing and Purchasing Natural Wines

Project Background

Timeline

May 27, 2021 - June 24, 2021 | (6 weeks)

Tools

My Role

Intensive solo student project and end-to-end case study for Career Foundry UI/UX bootcamp with the guidance of my tutor and mentor.

Objective

I designed an app that connects users to a marketplace for only natural wines. Users can order directly from the application and also scan wine labels to determine if they are natural wine or regular wine.

Problem

There is a lot of confusion about natural wine and no government issued label to identify natural wines.

Natural wine is complicated but in general terms it is farmed organically (biodynamically) and made without adding or removing anything during the winemaking process.

Solution

Feel Good Natural Wines does all the hard work and research into winemakers for users and guarantees a supply of only natural wines.

  • Users can browse and purchase natural wines and also scan a wine bottle to determine if it is a natural wine match.
  • I designed a set of 20 screens for the iOS app and a set of 20 screens for the Android app. Both apps have working prototypes.

Project Retrospective

What Did You Learn?

The focus of this project was on native applications involving Human Interface Guidelines (ios devices) and Google Material Design Guidelines (Android Devices). I also learned how to apply haptic feedback and sound during the wireframing process.

What Challenges Did You Encounter?

The most challenging part was learning about how iOS and Android native applications have different design approaches. I kept the design projects for the iOS and Android separate which helped with the organization. However, it was a lot of material to comb through. Overall, I learned a lot about the benefits of using a native application as opposed to other types of applications.

What Are the Next Steps?

The next steps would involve sharing the digital design assets with engineers to bring Feel Good Natural Wines to life. I would recruit more users to conduct further user testing.
