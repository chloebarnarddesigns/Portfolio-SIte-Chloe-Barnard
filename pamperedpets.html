Pampered Pals

A Online Pet Supply Store for Busy Pet Owners

Project Background

Timeline

June 19, 2021 - Aug. 31, 2021 | (6 weeks)

Tools

My Role

Intensive solo student project and end-to-end case study for Career Foundry UI/UX bootcamp with the guidance of my tutor and mentor.

Objective

I designed a responsive e-commerce website catering to the pet supplies sector which is the second largest consumer product group (CPG) in e-commerce and continues to outpace brick and mortar stores.

Problem

Pampered Pets targets shoppers looking for high quality dog and cat products. It congregates everything in one place for pet owners who don’t have time to make trips to physical stores.

Solution

I designed 18 final screens, tablet, and desktop designs corresponding to 4 mobile designs, and a clickable prototype.

Project Retrospective

What Did You Learn?

I learned how to design realistic mock credit card designs in Adobe XD on my own. I also learned more animations techniques to incorporate into prototypes. For instance, the users who have a registered account can swipe through their saved credit cards and pick the desired card.

What Challenges Did You Encounter?

This was my first project designing detailed filter and sort functions. It was challenging to figure out how best to display this information for the mobile first design. Another challenge was figuring out how to provide multiple payment options without confusing the user and without requiring the user to make an account.

What are the Next Steps?

The next step is to share the design assets with engineers. I would recruit users to conduct further usability testing.
