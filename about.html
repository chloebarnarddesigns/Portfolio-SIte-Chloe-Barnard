About

My Story

I research, ideate, and create interactive digital user experiences that are enjoyable, accessible, and playful. I previously worked for 2.5 years as a Gift Coordinator and Gift Analyst in the Office of Alumni Development and Fundraising for Columbia University in NY, NY where I strengthened my skills in using sound judgement and discretion when troubleshooting interdepartmental, vendor, and donor challenges and anticipating gift processing bottlenecks. This elevated my aptitude and understanding of providing customer service and anticipating stakeholders needs.

My curiosity in curating and improving people’s experiences combined with my research centric undergraduate background in Public Policy at the UNC Chapel Hill and my affinity to visual design lead me to discover UX and UI design. When the Covid-19 pandemic began, I embarked on a boot camp where I transitioned into interactive design as a UI/UX Designer.
